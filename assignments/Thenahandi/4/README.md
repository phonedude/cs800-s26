## Description

This assignment involves writing a literature review of at least five papers in my research area (eye-tracking and gaze estimation). The literature review includes:
- **Introduction**: An overview of the research area and the common problem/motivation/technique that the papers address
- **Summaries**: A paragraph for each paper summarizing the main ideas
- **Synthesis**: A description of how the papers fit together and their relationship to the overall body of work
- **References**: A list of references generated using BibTeX with appropriate citations
