### Presentation Focus
The presentation explores three primary methods developed or utilized in the dissertation:
1.  **MarkovFit:** A framework using Markov Random Fields (MRF) for the global assembly of protein complexes into medium-to-low resolution cryo-EM maps.
2.  **VESPER:** A vector-based local space alignment method for density map comparison.
3.  **MAINMAST:** A *de novo* modeling approach that traces protein backbones directly from EM density maps using Minimum Spanning Trees (MST).
