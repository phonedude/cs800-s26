## Assignment Description
This assignment is a **literature review** of at least five papers on **Honeynets and Cyber Deception in Critical Infrastructure**. The review includes:

- **Introduction:** Overview of the research area, motivation, and rationale for paper selection.  
- **Summaries:** One paragraph per paper summarizing the main ideas, methods, and contributions.  
- **Synthesis:** Analysis of how the papers relate to each other and the broader research field.  
- **References:** BibTeX-formatted references cited in the summaries.  

The goal is to demonstrate understanding of foundational concepts, recent advances, and practical applications of honeynets in critical infrastructure networks.
