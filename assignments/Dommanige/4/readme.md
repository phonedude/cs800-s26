# Assignment 4 – Literature Review

**CS 800 – Research Methods (Spring 2026)**

This assignment focuses on writing a literature review of research papers in my research area: **Location and Semantic-based Self-Verification for Table Data Extraction using Vision Language Models**.

The goal of the assignment is to review and synthesize at least five research papers that address challenges related to extracting structured data from complex scientific tables. The literature review summarizes the problem, approach, and contributions of each paper and analyzes how these works relate to each other and to the broader research area.

The literature review is organized into four main sections:

* **Introduction** – provides an overview of the research area and explains the motivation for selecting the papers.
* **Summaries** – contains a paragraph summary for each selected paper describing the main ideas.
* **Synthesis** – explains how the papers relate to each other and identifies the research gap in this area.
* **References** – generated using **BibTeX** based on the citations used in the summaries.

The document is written entirely in my own words and compiled as a **PDF using LaTeX** following the formatting requirements provided in the Overleaf template.
