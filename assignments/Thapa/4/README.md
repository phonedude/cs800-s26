This submission contains a literature review of five papers in my research area (applied cryptography, data security and privacy). The review contains the following sections:

* **Introduction** - one paragraph overview of the research area that is reviewed and the common problem/motivation/technique/dataset that the papers are addressing.

* **Synthesis** - a description of how the papers fit together and their relationship to the overall body of work.
* **References**- list of references included in the review.
