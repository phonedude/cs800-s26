# Assignment 4: Literature Review

**CS 800 Research Methods, Spring 2026**

## Description

This assignment is a literature review of five papers in my research area of
**graph neural network expressiveness and generalization via graph homomorphism**.
The review includes an Introduction, per-paper Summaries, a Synthesis section,
and a BibTeX-generated reference list. The document is typeset in LaTeX using
12 pt Times New Roman, 1-inch margins, and a running header containing the
assignment number and last name.

The five papers reviewed are:

1. **Barceló et al. (2021)** — *Graph Neural Networks with Local Graph Parameters* (NeurIPS 2021)
2. **Zhang et al. (2024)** — *Beyond Weisfeiler-Lehman: A Quantitative Framework for GNN Expressiveness* (ICLR 2024, Oral)
3. **Li et al. (2024)** — *Generalization of Graph Neural Networks through the Lens of Homomorphism* (arXiv 2024)
4. **Li et al. (2025)** — *Towards Bridging Generalization and Expressivity of Graph Neural Networks* (ICLR 2025)
5. **Zhang et al. (2026)** — *Reinterpreting Hypergraph Kernels: Insights Through Homomorphism Analysis* (IEEE TPAMI 2026)

These are the same papers used in Assignment 3.
